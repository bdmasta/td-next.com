TD next, a pioneer in well-known innovations

TD next has over 15 years experience in developing M2M or IoT projects

  • 1996-2000

    Atlantic network systems

    External multi connect dongles for laptops (PSTN, ISDN, LAN, GSM DATA). 500K units sold / $M12 (2000)!

  • 2001-2005

    KirrioTM

    GPS car kits & GPS PND. 500K units sold / $M50 (2005)

    KirrioTM has been one of the central players behind the emergence of GPS navigation for the consumer market. Major stakeholders such as Palm and HP have adopted the Kirrio technology and products, before it was acquired by Michelin Group in 2005.

  • 2000-2009

    Homerider systems

    Global AMR solution for meters, tanks, containers. Sold $M15 (2009)

    This truly technologically challenging project enabled a large-scale deployment of low consumption cellular urban networks in order to control water meters, waste containers and billboards...the Smart City was born! Homerider was acquired by Veolia in 2009

  • 2011-today

    First partnership with SIGFOX

    Sigfox RF modules and Cloud on ChipTM technology

    TD next realized that existing IoT solutions came up against several key issues: the complexity of implementation, cost & energy consumption. Those obstacles prevented billion passive objects from being connected and used by high-end consumers. However, TD next developped a technology to overcome those issues, by relying on a strong strategic partnership with SIGFOX, 1st UNB network for IoT.
